Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by selecting a doctor and choosing an available time slot on our website.

What if I need to cancel my appointment?

You can cancel your appointment through your account on our website or by contacting our support team.

Do I need to create an account to book an appointment?

Yes, creating an account will help you manage your appointments easily and receive notifications.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring a valid ID, insurance information (if applicable), and any relevant medical records.

Can I reschedule my appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule your appointment through your account or by contacting our support team.

What if I arrive late for my appointment?

If you arrive late, please inform the receptionist. Depending on the schedule, you may have to wait or reschedule.

Is telemedicine available?

Yes, we offer telemedicine appointments. You can select this option while booking your appointment.
