Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if my appointment is confirmed?

After booking, you will see your appointment listed in your dashboard. You will also receive an email confirming your appointment.

Can I leave feedback for a doctor?

Yes, after your appointment, you will have the option to leave feedback and a rating for the doctor.

What if I need to cancel my appointment?

You can cancel your appointment through your account on our webiste.

Do I need to create an account to book an appointment?

Yes, creating an account will help you manage your appointments easily and receive notifications.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring a valid ID, insurance information (if applicable), and any relevant medical records.

What happens if I miss my appointments?

If you miss 4 appointments, you will receive a warning email. On the 5th missed appointment, your account will be temporarily blocked for 3 days. After this period, your account will be automatically unblocked and you can book appointments again.

How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by logging into your account, choosing a doctor, selecting a date and time, and confirming the booking.
